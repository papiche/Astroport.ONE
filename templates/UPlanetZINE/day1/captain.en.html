Weekly economic cycle

Each week, the system automatically:

  • Collects: MULTIPASS 1 Zen + ZEN Card 4 Zen (+ VAT 20%)
  • Pays the PAF: 1x PAF Shipowner + 2x PAF Captain
  • Allocates surplus: 33% Treasury + 33% R&D + 33% Assets

Script: ZEN.COOPERATIVE.3x1-3.sh (triggered on captain anniversary)

Onboarding users

To create a user MULTIPASS:

  • Web: the user goes to your UPassport and scans the QR
  • CLI: ./tools/make_NOSTRCARD.sh email lang lat lon
  • ZEN Card: created automatically by VISA.new.sh

The user receives an email with their ZEN Card (day0) and the following ZINEs automatically.

Restoration and migration

If a user arrives from another station:

  • With .pass: ./tools/nostr_RESTORE_TW.sh CID_ZIP
  • Without .pass (same UPlanet): ./tools/nostr_RESTORE_TW.sh CID_UPLANET
  • The G1 cashback is sent back automatically (minus 1 G1 primo)
  • The secret.june history (cooperative shares) is preserved

If different UPlanet: .pass mandatory, no cashback.
