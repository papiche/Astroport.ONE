Day 1: Your digital space is active

Your ZEN Card is working. Here is what is already running for you, 24/7.

What is active now

  • Your sovereign identity: a unique key that identifies you on Nostr, the G1 blockchain and IPFS. No password to remember.
  • Your storage space (uDRIVE): 128 GB private, accessible from any device.
  • Your automatic journal: Bot Astro collects messages from your friends and creates a daily journal for you.
  • Your ZEN wallet: you start with the primo TX. Each like you receive on your publications = 1 Zen.

How it works day to day

1. You publish on Nostr (via Coracle, Amethyst, or any Nostr client).

2. Your friends see 100% of your messages, in chronological order. No algorithm.

3. The likes you receive are automatically converted into Zen on your wallet.

4. Bot Astro creates a journal for you with publications from your constellation (friends + friends of friends).

Everything is automatic. You have nothing to configure.

Your fee: 5 Zen / week

Each week, your wallet is automatically debited:

  • 1 Zen MULTIPASS: access to basic services
  • 4 Zen ZEN Card: cooperator, NextCloud, voting rights

Your account started at 0 Zen. If in 7 days you do not have at least 5 Zen, your account will be deactivated (except Captain).

Quick solution: publish 5 messages that each receive 1 like. Or become a Sponsor (50 euros/year = no more weekly fees).

Where do the Zen go?

Each collected Zen is automatically distributed by the code (not by a human):

  • 1/3 Treasury: operating reserve for sustainability
  • 1/3 R&D: free software development
  • 1/3 Commons: acquisition of land, forests, infrastructure

The Shipowner (host) and the Captain (operator) are compensated through this same economy.

Full transparency: all transactions are visible on the G1 blockchain.

Your data belongs to you

  • Zero tracking: no advertising, no profiling.
  • Zero censorship: your cryptographic keys guarantee that nobody can delete your publications.
  • Portable: if you switch stations, your data migrates with you (MULTIPASS + uDRIVE).
  • Recoverable: your identity is protected by Shamir's secret (SSSS 2/3). Even if you lose your card, you can recover your account.
