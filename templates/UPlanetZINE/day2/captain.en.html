Captain Day 2: Grow your garden

Your station is active. Now you need to make it viable and grow it.

Viability threshold

Your station costs 3x PAF/week (1x Shipowner + 2x Captain). To cover this cost:

  • If PAF = 14 Zen: you need 42 Zen/week in revenue
  • With MULTIPASS at 1 Zen + ZEN Card at 4 Zen = 5 Zen/user
  • Threshold: ~9 ZEN Card users to cover the PAF

Dashboard: economy.html shows the balance in real time.

Recruiting Sponsors

Sponsors fund the hardware and free users from the weekly fee:

  • Satellite (50 EUR/year): share of a Raspberry Pi. NextCloud 128 GB + vote.
  • Constellation (540 EUR/3 years): share of a GPU server. AI video + priority.

Links configurable in economy.Swarm.html > EDIT > OpenCollective URLs.

Each Sponsor = 1 user who no longer costs anything in weekly fees.

Finding a Shipowner

The Shipowner hosts the machine at their place. They provide:

  • A secure physical space
  • A 24/7 fiber connection
  • Electricity
  • A reboot in case of failure

In exchange: 1x PAF/week (14 Zen by default). Hosting contract with G1FabLab.

The hardware remains property of the collective.

The rule of thirds in practice

When the CAPTAIN_DEDICATED surplus > 0, the 3x1/3 allocation kicks in:

  • 33% Treasury: reserve for contingencies, hardware replacement
  • 33% R&D: funding open source development
  • 33% Assets: acquisition of land and forests for the collective
  • +1% Captain Bonus: performance premium

Script: ZEN.COOPERATIVE.3x1-3.sh (automatic, captain anniversary)

Management tools

  • captain.sh: complete CLI dashboard (status, players, economy)
  • cooperative_config.sh: edit cooperative config via DID NOSTR
  • uplanet_onboarding.sh: onboarding and configuration
  • command.sh: station CLI interface

Documentation: CLAUDE.md in the Astroport.ONE repo
