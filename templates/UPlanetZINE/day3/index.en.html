Day 3: Your own feed

Facebook decides who sees your posts.
On UPlanet, it is the opposite: your friends see 100% of your messages. No algorithm. No sorting. No ads.

This is the N2 Protocol: my friends' friends are my friends.

How it works

1. You publish on your Astroport station (your personal node).

2. Your message propagates to your friends' stations (N1).

3. Then to your friends' friends (N2): this is your constellation.

4. Everyone sees everything, in chronological order.

No need to "like" to be visible. No algorithm hiding your messages. This is a real social network.

Bi-annual journal & MULTIPASS on web2

Bot Astro generates your six-month review and your AI acts on the web on your behalf:

  • Bi-annual journal: summary of your constellation, published on your IPNS twice a year
  • YouTube: your liked videos copied automatically into your uDRIVE (/cookie)
  • Any web2 site: delegate your cookies via /cookie — the system extends to all services

Export cookies from a private/incognito window then upload them. Your AI acts, your data stays in your MULTIPASS.

Smart geolocation

UPlanet divides the planet into zones with automatic Nostr identities:

  • UMAP (1 km): everything happening in your neighborhood
  • SECTOR (11 km): messages with 3+ likes from your zone
  • REGION (111 km): popular content (12+ likes) from your region

Quality content naturally rises to broader zones. Zero manipulation: this is content meritocracy.

Each like = 1 Zen

On Facebook, a like earns you nothing. On UPlanet:

  • Each like (reaction kind 7) you receive = 1 Zen credited automatically to your wallet.
  • Publish quality content, share useful information, and your activity funds your fee.
  • No ads, no sponsoring: the value comes from the community.

Your attention has value. Here, you are the one who benefits.

AI that works for you

The #BRO protocol gives you access to a local AI, shared across stations:

  • Send #BRO summarize my messages for a summary of your constellation
  • Send #BRO create an image of... to generate visuals
  • The AI runs on the cooperative's machines, not on Big Tech servers
  • Your data is never used to profile you

Invite your friends

The network grows naturally:

  • Each friend you invite joins your N1 constellation.
  • Their friends become your N2: your network expands automatically.
  • No limit: the more the network grows, the more resilient it becomes.
  • If a station goes down, the network continues. Data is replicated.
